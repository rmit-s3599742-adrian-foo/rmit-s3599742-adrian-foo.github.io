Hello This Is Adrian

This is an introduction of myself, I hope you enjoy it. am a third year student in RMIT University. I was born in Malaysia, but settled in Australia since a young age. I speak around two different langauges, Chinese and English. Here are two interesting facts about Adrian.

  • One, Adrian loves pets and he actually have two dogs residing in his hometown, Malaysia. Who the two dogs happen to be twins.
  • Second, Adrian loves watching media of all kinds from Movies and more. As he grew up watching films on family nights every weekend night going to the cinema to watch films as a family.

Interest in IT

My interest in it started when I first used a computer in my home. I was only 10 years old when I got to use it and the experience interacting with my first computer sparked my interest instantly. It was my curiosity in the technology that sparked my interest as I said and that made me purse to know everything that made the computer worked. Specifically hardware. So that’s how my curiosity in IT begin.


My area of interest currently in IT are around A.I and automation how the computer can start and end flows of work when given instruction and the fact when it comes to A.I. How it could make decision on it’s own excites me about the possibilities and its application.


My current experience in IT are programming languages like Python, Web programming and Java. Additionally some experience working with hardware from building up a PC from scratch.

Why did I choose RMIT?

I chose RMIT because it was the closest to where I live which is in the city of Melbourne, and I like the guidance and impression the teacher gave to me when I came to open day when I was planning for my university degree. The guidance and helpfulness of the teacher really helped me in that instance make an important decision, therefore, I decided I want to study IT at RMIT university on the same day.

what do you expect to learn during your studies?

I expect to learn during my studies these 3 areas. Firstly, to learn the latest technology being used in the industry. Secondly, the skills to operate as an IT professional . Lastly, it’s the market demand for IT technology and it’s application of IT in different industries.
